Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Overview

Good Pizza, Great Burger ("the Game") is developed by Vedat Kocyigit ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what information is collected when you play the Game, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information ourselves. All game progress is saved locally on your device only.

Information We Collect

The Game does not require account registration or any personal data from you. Gameplay data such as your money, stock, and progress is stored locally on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs and is never transmitted to our servers.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personal identifiers.

Advertising (Unity Ads)

The Game displays rewarded video advertisements provided by Unity Technologies ("Unity Ads"). Unity Ads may collect certain device information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • Device model, operating system version, and language
  • IP address and general geographic region
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

Unity Ads' data practices are governed by the Unity Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14 and later, the Game will ask for your permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. This permission controls whether Unity Ads can access your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA).

  • If you allow tracking: Unity Ads may show you more relevant advertisements based on your interests.
  • If you decline: Unity Ads will still show ads, but they will not be personalized. No other functionality of the Game is affected.

You can change this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Data Retention

Game progress data is stored exclusively on your device and is removed when you uninstall the Game. We have no access to this data. Data collected by Unity Ads is subject to Unity's own retention policies.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our services, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Game after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
